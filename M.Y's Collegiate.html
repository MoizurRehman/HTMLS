Welcome To M.Y's Collegiate

The new standard in American curriculum schools .
The Collegiate American School offers the best of American education through a program inspired by the best practices in curriculum, assessment and instruction. With a curriculum based on the American Common Core Standards and a technology-enabled learning environment focusing on digital learning, the Collegiate American School helps students develop the skills and knowledge to compete throughout the world. The Collegiate American School has been created by Innoventures Education, the driving force behind successful educational institutions in Dubai including the Dubai International Academy and the Raffles International Schools and Nurseries, Dubai.
